# Go for Dhilan!

This is the launchpad for all-things-Dhilan.  Visit the [blog](\blog) for the latest, stream of consciousness thoughts.

### Dhilan is currently:
> *	teaching math to remote fourth graders at [John Hay Elementary](https://jhes.hcboe.net/) back home in Tennessee
* redesigning a physics (Physics 123) and comp-sci (CS141) course
* developing [TinyMLx](https://www.edx.org/professional-certificate/harvardx-tiny-machine-learning) for wider access to embedded AI
* planning an embedded hardware workshop in coordination with [Navajo Technical University](http://www.navajotech.edu/) for students in the Navajo Nation, using our [TinyML Arduino kit](https://store.arduino.cc/usa/tiny-machine-learning-kit)
* producing interstitials for [EastTN PBS](https://www.easttennesseepbs.org/)
* studying Hungarian mathematics through St. Olaf College in Budapest
